FAQs

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Is the domain and hosting really free — no hidden charges? +
Yes, 100% free. Your domain name (your choice of .co.za, .com, .online, etc.) is registered at no cost for 1 year. Your hosting — including active administration, security monitoring, backups, and support — is also fully covered for 12 months. After year 1, normal hosting rates apply (from R79/mo). No surprises.
What does "administered hosting" actually mean? +
Standard hosting means you get server space but manage everything yourself. Administered hosting means our team actively manages it for you — performing daily backups, applying CMS and plugin updates, monitoring uptime, scanning for malware, fixing errors, and optimising performance. Think of it as having a part-time IT team looking after your site.
How long does it take to build my website? +
Starter and Business Pro sites typically go live within 5–7 business days. E-Commerce stores take 10–14 days depending on the number of products. Enterprise / custom projects are scoped individually. Timelines can be faster when you provide content and images promptly.
Do I need to provide my own content and images? +
We always prefer your own brand content and photos, as they perform better. However, if you don't have images, we use high-quality, licensed stock photography at no additional charge. We can also assist with basic copywriting for your pages. Business and E-Commerce packages include more content assistance.
What happens after the free year of hosting ends? +
At the end of year 1, you can continue with one of our hosted plans starting from R79/mo. We'll contact you at least 30 days in advance to arrange renewal. You are never locked in — if you choose to move your website elsewhere, we'll assist with the transfer. Your domain is yours to keep.
Can I update my website content myself after launch? +
Yes! We build sites on WordPress with a simple, user-friendly admin panel. We walk you through how to update text, images, and blog posts during handover. If you'd prefer us to make updates for you, we offer affordable monthly maintenance add-ons.
Do you work with businesses outside South Africa? +
Absolutely. While our primary market is South Africa, we serve clients across Africa and internationally. Pricing is in ZAR but we accept international payments. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept EFT bank transfers, PayFast, credit/debit card, and cash deposits. A 50% deposit is required to commence work, with the balance payable before go-live. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects — ask us about this.
